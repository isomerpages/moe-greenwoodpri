<p><strong>A Warm Welcome from Day One</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We start building connections before the school year even begins. In November,
incoming P1 students join us for orientation to explore the school and
meet their teachers. In December, every child receives a personal welcome
letter from their form teacher. On the first day of school in January,
the whole school comes together to welcome your child. Staff, student leaders
and P4 Buddies guide our youngest learners through their new environment.</p>
<p>The first week is spent teaching routines together, because we never assume
children know how things work — we teach, model and practise every step
of the way.</p>
<p>At the end of Term 1, we celebrate every child's growth through <strong>Celebrating My Milestones</strong> —
because we believe in recognising effort, resilience and progress, not
just grades.</p>

<p><strong>Wonder in the Woods</strong>
</p>
<p>Our lower primary library is designed with the 6 to 9 year old in mind,
featuring three purposeful spaces — a Reading Space with age-appropriate
books across genres, a Collaborative Space for interdisciplinary activities
and Maker Space projects during recess, and a Self-expression Space with
a stage for storytelling, Show-and-Tell and dramatisation.</p>
<p>Beyond serving as a space for exploration and the quenching of curiosity,
the library also stands as a haven for contemplation. It provides visitors
with an opportunity to pause, reflect, and glean insights from their exploratory
journeys and adventures.</p>
